About Me

I am Nishi Agrawal, the founder of Panna Mehndi Designs, with over 5 years of experience in the art of mehndi. Over the years, I have had the privilege of teaching more than 1,000 students, helping them master the skill of mehndi art. In addition to offering mehndi design services, I also sell high-quality mehndi products and provide professional nail extension classes. My passion is to share my knowledge and skills with others, empowering them to start their own creative ventures in this vibrant field.

Feel free to contact me for collaborations or just to chat about tech!
